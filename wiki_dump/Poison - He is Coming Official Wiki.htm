If you have no armor, take damage equal to poison at turn start. Remove 1 stack at turn start.

Mechanics

  • At the start of your turn, if you have 0 armor, you take damage equal to your current Poison stacks.
  • Remove 1 Poison stack at the start of your turn.
  • Poison is part of the Status effect tags.
